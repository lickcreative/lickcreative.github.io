75% of all smartphones worldwide are expected to contain an NFC chip by the end of the decade.

Glossary

NFC is a simple, intuitive technology that lets consumers interact securely with the world around them with simple touches or taps. There's no need to launch download or open an app - customers can just tap POS to be taken to bespoke mobile experiences or to quickly make transactions.
