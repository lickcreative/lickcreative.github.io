About Us

We ideate, design and deliver digital products and services that connect and engage. Our multi-disciplinary team develop immersive consumer experiences across multiple industries. We collaborate with our clients, supplying the thinking behind the content that satisfies audience needs and surpasses business goals

Our Work

Planes 2

An interactive digital experience that puts the player into the heart of a game featuring the Planes characters. Using Kinetic gesture recognition technology, players control a plane by their arm movements. The experience toured around key cinema sites within the UK generating awareness of the movie, increasing footfall and ticket sales.

Transformers 4

To promote Michael Bay’s latest installment in the Transformers franchise we created an activation zone centred around the world famous Carlton Hotel. Festival visitors and the media were met with giant theatrical elements surrounding the entrance to the hotel featuring the key characters and movie title. Massive standout was further enhanced with a huge digital screen showcasing the movie to the massed crowds.

Tesco

To provide increased awareness of Tesco products and services we have created engaging animations shown at strategic points in store. The animations bring the products to life and provide a level of engagement above static POS, allowing for greater penetration of content to the consumer and increase dwell times. We successfully assisted in the launch of the Tesco Bank current account which also saw brand ambassadors equipped with animations loaded onto Hudl devices for a more personal engagement.

Adidas

To support the launch of the brand new Adidas Manchester store and celebrate the pedestrianisation of Oxford Street over the Christmas period we brought the streets alive. The experiential activity included living windows, street dancing, silent discos and in-store DJs. Onlookers were invited to partake in the silent disco and dance for a free pair of trainers. Brand ambassadors were on hand to provide demonstrations of Micoach and help drive traffic into store. In addition we created all the digital assets for email, web and social channels, including a content plan of pre and post event activity.
