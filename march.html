March

52% of the top 100 US retailers are testing beacons in stores this year.

A recent report finds that these small devices are the fastest-growing in-store technology since mobile card readers. The beacon install-base will see triple digit growth over the next few years.

Source: the Beacons Report, Business Insider UK 2014

Glossary

Beacons are "Bluetooth Smart" devices each emitting a unique code. They are battery powered, lasting around 2 years, and have a range of up to 50m. If the unique code is embedded within an app and someone with that app installed on their Bluetooth enabled phone enters the predetermined range their phone will recognise the beacon and can send the user a notification or perform an action.
