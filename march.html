52% of the top 100 US retailers are testing beacons in stores this year.

A recent report finds that these small devices are the fastest-growing in-store technology since mobile card readers. The beacon install-base will see triple digit growth over the next few years.

Source: the Beacons Report, Business Insider UK 2014

Glossary

Beacons are 'Bluetooth Smart' devices that can interact with nearby smartphones.They are battery powered, last around 2 years and have a range of up to 50m. Each beacon emits a unique code that is embedded within an app. When someone with that app installed on their smartphone enters the predetermined range (with bluetooth enabled) their phone will recognise the beacon's code and can send the user a notification or perform an action.
